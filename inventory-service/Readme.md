# Inventory Microservice

Welcome to the Inventory Microservice! This microservice is a part of our larger web application, employing a microservices architecture to efficiently manage inventory-related functionalities.

## Overview

The Inventory Microservice is responsible for handling and managing product inventory within our system. It ensures accurate tracking, updates, and availability of products across the platform.

## Features

- **Product Management:** Add, update, and delete products in the inventory.
- **Inventory Tracking:** Real-time tracking of product quantities and availability.
- **Search and Filter:** Efficiently search and filter products based on various criteria.
- **API Integration:** Seamlessly integrates with other microservices through well-defined APIs.
